COUNTRIES THAT I WILL VISIT IN THE FUTURE

Many travelers and backpackers have spent a great deal of their time and money traveling around the world, and some people are very doubtful about why those travelers and backpackers do so. Actually, the more you travel, the more you will realize there are just so many places more to visit. That is simply because the world, by its nature, is very beautiful, and some places will form a unique exciting experience and memory for the travelers.

IRELAND

Ireland is an island nation on the westernmost edge of Europe. It is the continent's second largest island (after Great Britain).

NORWAY

Norway is a narrow country in northern Europe. It shares the Scandinavian Peninsula with Sweden and Finland.

ZEALAND

New Zealand is a remote, mountainous group of islands in the southeastern Pacific Ocean.
